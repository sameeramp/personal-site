Trimly – Video Trimming & Editing Tool

Trimly is a simple static website built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, allowing users to upload and trim videos with ease. The app provides a responsive interface to trim video clips, remove unwanted sections, and save them in various formats. Built with a focus on simplicity and user-friendliness, Trimly offers a smooth and quick trimming experience. Whether you're editing a personal video for social media or trimming a longer video for a project, Trimly is the go-to solution for anyone needing a fast, no-frills video editor.
